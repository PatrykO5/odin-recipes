Bolognese

Description

This is the super easy recipe for the bolognese I make on a weekly basis when I don't have much time

Creates about 4 servings and takes 20-30 mins

Ingredients:

  • Dolmio sauce (one jar)
  • 500g mince beef
  • Any pasta (enough for 4 people)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves (can add more)
  • Worcester sauce
  • Cumin
  • Corriander
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheese

Optional:

  • Fresh basil
  • Carrot
  • Spinach

Method:

  1. Start boiling pasta (salted water)
  2. Fry onions for 3-4 mins, add crushed garlic for 1 min (add carrot at the same time as onion if using it)
  3. Add beef, break up nicely and mix with onions etc. Soon as mixed, add the spices and Worcester sauce
  4. Once beef browned completely add Dolmio sauce. Stir and watch until pasta is cooked (at least 5 mins, can be left as long as needed)
  5. Apparently can add dark chocolate whilst sauce is being done!
  6. Serve on pasta with grated cheese on top
